# ISMS Scope Statement

This document defines the scope of {{.Name}}'s Information Security Management System (ISMS) in accordance with ISO 27001:2022 Clause 4.3.

# Scope

The ISMS covers all information assets, systems, people, and processes involved in the delivery of {{.Name}}'s products and services, including:

a. All production infrastructure, cloud environments, and internally hosted systems used to process or store customer and organisational data.

a. All employees and contractors who access, process, or are responsible for the organisation's information assets.

a. All third-party suppliers and service providers with access to {{.Name}}'s systems or data, to the extent defined in applicable supplier agreements.

# Exclusions

The following are explicitly excluded from the scope of the ISMS:

a. Systems and infrastructure operated entirely by third parties under their own ISO 27001 certification, where {{.Name}} has no administrative access.

# Boundaries

The ISMS boundary encompasses all office locations and remote working environments from which {{.Name}} employees and contractors access organisational systems.

# Justification

This scope has been determined by considering the internal and external context of the organisation as documented in the risk assessment process, and the requirements and expectations of interested parties including customers, regulators, and employees.
