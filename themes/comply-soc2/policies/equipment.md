# Purpose

This policy establishes requirements for the siting, protection, maintenance, and disposal of equipment used by {{.Name}} to reduce the risk of unauthorised access, damage, and information loss.

# Scope

This policy applies to all equipment owned or operated by {{.Name}}, including servers, workstations, network devices, mobile devices, and any hardware used to process or store organisational information.

# Equipment Siting and Protection

Equipment is sited to minimise unnecessary access to work areas and to protect against environmental threats and hazards. Server and networking equipment is located in designated secure areas with appropriate physical access controls. Equipment is protected from power failures using uninterruptible power supplies (UPS) and surge protection. Cabling is protected from interception or damage and is routed or managed in accordance with technical standards.

# Equipment Maintenance

All equipment is maintained in accordance with manufacturer recommendations and applicable service agreements to ensure availability and integrity. Maintenance is carried out only by authorised personnel or approved third-party service providers. Records of maintenance activities are retained. Where equipment is sent offsite for maintenance, any sensitive information is removed or protected prior to transfer, and the equipment is tracked throughout the process.

# Secure Disposal and Re-use of Equipment

Before disposal or re-use, all equipment containing storage media is checked to ensure that any sensitive data and licensed software have been removed or securely overwritten. Storage devices containing sensitive information are physically destroyed or securely wiped using approved methods that prevent recovery. The disposal or transfer of equipment is documented and approved. Equipment containing sensitive data must not be disposed of without written authorisation from the asset owner and Information Security team.
