# Statement of Applicability

This document constitutes the Statement of Applicability (SoA) for {{.Name}}'s Information Security Management System (ISMS), as required by ISO 27001:2022 Clause 6.1.3(d).

# Purpose

The SoA records which Annex A controls have been determined to be applicable to the organisation, the justification for their inclusion or exclusion, and whether each applicable control has been implemented.

# Applicability Determination

The selection of controls is based on the results of the organisation's information security risk assessment and risk treatment process. Controls are included where they address one or more identified risks, or where they are required to meet legal, regulatory, or contractual obligations.

# Control Implementation Status

All 93 controls defined in ISO 27001:2022 Annex A have been evaluated. The applicability and implementation status of each control is maintained in the organisation's risk treatment plan and reviewed annually.

Controls determined to be not applicable are documented with justification in the full SoA register, which is maintained separately and made available to auditors upon request.

# Exclusions

Where Annex A controls have been determined to be not applicable, this is because the associated risk does not exist within the scope of the ISMS, or because the risk has been accepted or transferred in accordance with the risk treatment plan.

# Review

This statement is reviewed at least annually, or following significant changes to the organisation's risk profile, business operations, or the ISO 27001 standard itself.
