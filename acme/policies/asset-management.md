# Purpose

This policy establishes requirements for the identification, ownership, and lifecycle management of information assets at {{.Name}}, including their return upon termination of employment or contract.

# Scope

This policy applies to all information assets owned or used by {{.Name}}, including hardware, software, data, and services, and to all personnel who use or manage those assets.

# Asset Inventory

{{.Name}} maintains an inventory of all assets associated with information and information processing facilities. Each asset in the inventory includes: asset type, owner, location, classification, and lifecycle status. The inventory is reviewed and updated at least annually and whenever significant changes occur. Asset owners are responsible for ensuring their assets are included in the inventory and that classification is accurate.

# Asset Ownership

Every asset has a designated owner who is accountable for its appropriate management and protection. Asset owners are responsible for ensuring the asset is used appropriately, classified correctly, and that access rights are maintained in line with the access control policy.

# Return of Assets

All employees, contractors, and third-party users are required to return all organisational assets in their possession upon termination of employment, contract, or agreement. This includes physical devices, access credentials, documents, and any other organisational property. The offboarding process includes a formal checklist to confirm return of all assets prior to access being revoked. Where assets cannot be returned, the asset owner and Information Security team must be notified and appropriate action taken.
