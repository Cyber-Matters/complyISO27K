# Purpose

This policy establishes pre-employment screening requirements to ensure that {{.Name}} verifies the suitability of candidates for roles with access to sensitive information or systems.

# Scope

This policy applies to all prospective employees, contractors, and third-party personnel who will be granted access to {{.Name}} information systems or facilities.

# Pre-Employment Screening

Background verification checks are carried out on all candidates for employment in accordance with applicable laws, regulations, and ethical requirements. Checks are proportional to the sensitivity of the role and the information to which the individual will have access.

Screening may include, as appropriate to the role:

- Verification of identity (e.g., government-issued photo ID)
- Confirmation of claimed educational and professional qualifications
- Employment history verification for the preceding five years
- Criminal record check where permitted by local law
- Credit or financial background check for roles with access to financial systems

Screening must be completed and results reviewed before access is granted. Any adverse findings are assessed in context and escalated to HR and the hiring manager for a decision. Offers of employment may be withdrawn if screening reveals material misrepresentation or disqualifying information.

# Contractor and Third-Party Personnel

Where screening is performed by a third-party supplier, {{.Name}} requires written confirmation that screening has been conducted to equivalent standards. This requirement is included in supplier contracts and reviewed as part of supplier management.
