# Purpose

This policy ensures that {{.Name}} identifies and meets its legal, statutory, regulatory, and contractual obligations relating to information security, integrates security into project management, and protects intellectual property rights.

# Scope

This policy applies to all projects, products, services, and operations of {{.Name}}, and to all personnel involved in the development, delivery, and management of those activities.

# Information Security in Project Management

Information security is integrated into {{.Name}}'s project management methodology. All projects must include an information security assessment at initiation to identify requirements and risks. Security controls are specified, implemented, and tested as part of the project lifecycle. Project managers are responsible for ensuring compliance with this policy. Security acceptance criteria are defined prior to project go-live.

# Legal, Statutory, Regulatory and Contractual Requirements

{{.Name}} identifies, documents, and keeps current all legal, statutory, regulatory, and contractual requirements relevant to information security for each of its information systems and services. Compliance obligations are reviewed at least annually and whenever changes in the regulatory environment are identified. Designated personnel are responsible for monitoring changes in applicable law and regulation and communicating requirements to affected teams.

# Intellectual Property Rights

{{.Name}} complies with applicable laws, regulations, and contractual requirements relating to intellectual property rights, including software licensing, copyright, trademarks, and patents. Only approved and properly licensed software may be installed on organisational systems. Employees are prohibited from reproducing, distributing, or using copyrighted material without appropriate authorisation. Suspected violations of intellectual property rights must be reported to the Legal function.
